# aws-news-agent

AI agent that assists with keeping up with AWS news and announcements


## Usage

```
AWS News Assistant (x to exit):  what's been happening with bedrock lately

Okay, let me check the latest AWS news on Bedrock:

Here are the key updates on Amazon Bedrock that I found:

- In December 2024, Amazon Bedrock announced the availability of Stable Diffusion 3.5 Large and Meta's Llama 3.3 70B model.
- Amazon Bedrock Guardrails now supports additional languages like Spanish and French, and has reduced pricing by up to 85%.
- Amazon Bedrock Model Evaluation is now available in the Europe (Zurich) region.
- Amazon Bedrock Knowledge Bases now supports multimodal data processing, structured data retrieval, and GraphRAG (preview).
- Amazon Bedrock is introducing new capabilities like Intelligent Prompt Routing, Prompt Caching, and Model Distillation (all in preview).
- Amazon Bedrock Marketplace now offers over 100 models for customers to use.
- Amazon Bedrock IDE is now available in preview as part of Amazon SageMaker Unified Studio.
- Amazon Nova foundation models are now available in Amazon Bedrock.
- Amazon Bedrock now supports multi-agent collaboration and latency-optimized inference for foundation models.

Let me know if you need any additional details on these Bedrock updates!

AWS News Assistant (x to exit):  tell me more about nova mdoels

Okay, let me dive deeper into the announcement about the Amazon Nova foundation models being made available in Amazon Bedrock:

The key details about the new Amazon Nova foundation models are:

- Amazon Nova is a new generation of state-of-the-art (SOTA) foundation models that deliver frontier intelligence and industry leading price performance.

- The Amazon Nova models available today in Amazon Bedrock are:
  - Amazon Nova Micro - a text-only model with low latency and low cost
  - Amazon Nova Lite - a low-cost multimodal model for fast processing of image, video, and text
  - Amazon Nova Pro - a highly capable multimodal model with the best balance of accuracy, speed, and cost
  - Amazon Nova Canvas - a state-of-the-art image generation model
  - Amazon Nova Reel - a state-of-the-art video generation model

- The Nova Micro, Lite, and Pro models are among the fastest and most cost-effective in their intelligence classes. They are optimized for RAG and agentic applications.

- You can fine-tune the Nova Micro, Lite, and Pro models on Amazon Bedrock to customize them for your specific needs.

- The Nova Canvas and Nova Reel models provide production-grade visual content with built-in controls for safe and responsible AI use.

- The Nova models are available in the US East (N. Virginia) region, and the Micro, Lite, and Pro models are also available in the US West (Oregon) and US East (Ohio) regions via cross-region inference.

- You can get started with the Amazon Nova foundation models from the Amazon Bedrock console.

Let me know if you need any other details on the new Amazon Nova models!
```
